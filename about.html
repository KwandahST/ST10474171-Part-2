About Us:

K's Ice Cream is a store that aims to create and serve delicious, frozen treats made with the grandest of ingrediants we source locally and all over the globe.

Our ice cream stores prides itself with individuality while also getting the basics right. Offering the classics such as chocolate and vanilla ice ream while also having specific flavours such as Sicilian pistachio pumpkin ice cream and many more making the customer's 'ice cream world' that much bigger.

Variety is imperative to K's Ice Cream because not everyone might be keen on ice cream that day so we also offer waffles, pancakes and various hot beverages for customers who are feeling a bit chilly. Vegan options to the majority of products we offer are available and are made with care and consideration to our fellow vegan customers.

Our target market icludes families, young adults, seniors and of course the children. Playful and sweet ice cream for the kids, classy and artsinal for the adults and we also offer sugar free ice cream for our health enthusiasts.

K's ice cream not only wants to offer the best treats at an affordable price, but be the backbone for the communtity it resides in, being a safe space that different kinds of people can create joyful memories for themselves and their loved ones. Making each visit a one to remember.
